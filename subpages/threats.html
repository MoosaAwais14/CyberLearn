What are

Threats refer to any malicious software that compromises the vulnerabilities in a
system to steal personal information, violate the CIA Triad, or cause damage to a system.

Threats Include...
  • Malware
    • Software that is used to damage or gain unauthorized access to a computer system.

  • Ransomware
    • A type of malicious software designed to deny access to a system until a certain amount of money - or ransom - is paid

  • Phishting
    • When fraudulent messages that are perceived to be credible trick users into giving up personal information.
To stay safe from viruses and any potential threats, it is important to update software,
use strong passwords, and implement firewalls and security software.
