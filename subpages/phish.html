Phishing occurs when criminals try to get users to open harmful links that could steal valuable information. Phishing attacks usually happen through email, text, or phone call, and are designed to look reputable.
    The Three Steps to Stay Safe from Phishing:
    • Confidentiality
      • Phishing messages usually include untrusted URLs, spelling/grammar errors, incorrect email addresses/subjects, and requests to send personal information.
      • Phishing messages are usually “too good to be true”
      • Look out for all signs of Phishing

    • Resist
      • Resist the urge to click on any links if phishing is likely
      • Report the attack via the toolbar or settings

    • Delete
      • Delete the message
      • Avoid any “unsubscribe” URLs
      • Block the sender
